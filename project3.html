Earthquake Awareness

What is an Earthquake?

An earthquake is a sudden and rapid shaking of the ground caused by the breaking and shifting of rock beneath the Earth's surface. This can cause damage to buildings and infrastructure, as well as injuries and fatalities.

Preventing Earthquake Damage

There are several steps that can be taken to minimize damage from earthquakes, including reinforcing buildings and infrastructure, securing loose items in homes and workplaces, and practicing earthquake drills.

Emergency Response

In the event of an earthquake, it is important to stay calm and follow emergency protocols. This may include seeking shelter in a safe location, avoiding elevators and heavy furniture, and staying away from windows and other hazards.
